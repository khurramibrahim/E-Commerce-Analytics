# Fuzzy Factory E-Commerce Performance Dashboard

## Overview
This Power BI report provides a comprehensive analysis of **Fuzzy Factory’s e-commerce performance**, focusing on website traffic, order growth, conversion efficiency, revenue trends, product performance, and cross-selling opportunities.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business growth, evaluate marketing channel effectiveness, and identify key drivers impacting sales and profitability.

---

## Business Problem
E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of customer interaction and sales data across multiple channels. Without centralized reporting, it becomes difficult to:
- Track order growth trends
- Measure conversion efficiency
- Analyze channel performance
- Monitor revenue and margins
- Evaluate product impact
- Identify cross-selling opportunities

This dashboard consolidates these insights into a single interactive reporting solution.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Main Page
Executive summary dashboard providing:
- Overall business performance overview
- Navigation to detailed analytical pages
- Key business KPIs and highlights

---

### 2. Order and Session Growth
Analysis of:
- Website sessions over time
- Order growth trends
- Traffic performance
- Customer engagement metrics

#### Key Insights
- Growth patterns in customer traffic
- Relationship between sessions and order volume
- Seasonal or campaign-driven spikes

---

### 3. Efficiency Improvements
Focuses on operational and conversion efficiency metrics.

#### Includes:
- Conversion trend analysis
- Performance improvement tracking
- Efficiency KPIs
- Time-based performance comparisons

---

### 4. Orders Through Different Channels
Breakdown of order performance across acquisition channels.

#### Channels May Include:
- Organic Search
- Paid Search
- Social Media
- Direct Traffic
- Referral Traffic

#### Analysis Includes:
- Channel contribution to orders
- Comparative channel performance
- Order distribution visualization

---

### 5. Session-to-Order Conversion by Channels
Analyzes how effectively traffic converts into orders across channels.

#### Metrics Included:
- Conversion rates
- Channel efficiency
- Customer journey effectiveness
- Traffic quality assessment

---

### 6. Revenue and Margins by Products
Product-level financial analysis focusing on:
- Revenue trends
- Product profitability
- Margin analysis
- Product performance comparison

---

### 7. Impact of New Products
Measures the business impact of newly launched products.

#### Analysis Includes:
- Revenue contribution
- Adoption trends
- Sales performance comparison
- Product growth impact

---

### 8. Product Cross-Sells
Cross-selling and customer purchase behavior analysis.

#### Insights Include:
- Frequently purchased product combinations
- Cross-sell opportunities
- Customer buying patterns
- Revenue optimization opportunities
